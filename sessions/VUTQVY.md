A quick tour of interesting places and anecdotes to explore on OpenStreetMap around the world🌍: because it’s beautiful, surprising, and delightfully crazy. Anything that makes you say, “Wooow!🫨 How?”🤔 or... “Why?🙃”, because that's definitely the kind of thing that leads people to OpenStreetMap! :)
Let's explore the (OSM) world!🤩

<hr>

Micromapping🔬, 3D buildings🏢, oddities🍿, from intensely mapped islands to polished amusement parks, including castles and their meticulously drawn gardens, all accompanied by a little compilation of map bloopers.

At the same time, a look at various map visualizations, apps that use OSM, editors, community tools, and community life. (openstreetmap.org, CoMaps, OsmAnd, cartes.app, JOSM, TagInfo, OSM Wiki, community forums, etc.)
