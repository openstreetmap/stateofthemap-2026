A few years ago, I gave a talk at [2022's State of the Map about the community behind OpenStreetMap](https://2022.stateofthemap.org/sessions/NWB9QF/) from an anthropologist's perspective, with a specific focus on the developments to the ecosystem since then. It asks questions: what has happened to satellite imagery? What are the crises that are constitutive of the community now? How have emerging markets affected mapping in the years since the original ethnographic study was organized? Building on the previous format of the talk given in 2022, this conference presentation will ask if the original premise of crises, communities, and capitalism still stands, a few years later.
